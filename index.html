Hi. You're looking at Symbiotes.

Venom

Venom is the first Symbiote to emerge from his home planet Klyntar. Living up to his name as the "Lethal Protector", Venom hunts to satisfy his bloodloust. When he is not hunting, he enjoys to sunbathe or curl on a couch when its gloomy outside.

Agony

Shortly after Venom's descent into Earth, Agony followed along. Agony is shy and mild-mannered. Don't be fooled by her shyness, She goes toe-to-toe with Riot. She puts the pur in perfect.

Riot

Riot is one of Venom's greatest adversary. Hungry for food, attention, and toys, Riot is a young Symbiote who chronically forgets she's still 4 weeks.

Moo Cow

Although, Moo was not officially a Symbiote, he was his brother to Venom. Moo enjoyed the finer things in life such as bananas, celery, apples, and assorted vegetables. He also enjoyed hanging out with his mother and even lets her do his makeup!

Bear

Bear is not officially a Symbiote. Instead, he is a dwarf rabbit with an insatiable appetite. He enjoys to spend time with Roo.
